## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Bicycle Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer demographics, and product trends.
The dashboard helps businesses understand sales distribution, customer behavior, and top-performing bike models.

It enables stakeholders to monitor performance and make data-driven decisions.

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to analyze bicycle sales data and answer key business questions:

What is the total revenue generated from bicycle sales?

Which bike models are selling the most?

Which age groups contribute the most to sales?

How do monthly, MTD, and YTD sales trends behave?

What is the gender contribution in total sales?
